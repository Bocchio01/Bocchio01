\cventry
{Academic project for Topology Optimization course}
{Topology Optimization of Automotive Hub Carrier}
{Politecnico di Milano, Milan (IT)}
{Jan 2025 - Feb 2025}
{
    Structural mass reduction study for a hub carrier component, balancing weight minimization with mechanical performance and manufacturing feasibility.
    \newline
    \vspace{4mm}
    \begin{cvitems}
        \item {Formulated optimization problem with compliance constraints and manufacturing restrictions (directional constant cross-section).}
        \item {Conducted iterative topology optimization using \textbf{Altair HyperWorks suite} to identify optimal material distribution.}
        \item {Analyzed stress concentrations and validated structural integrity under multiple load cases (braking, cornering, vertical impact).}
        \item {Generated manufacturable design proposals, evaluating trade-offs between mass reduction targets and production complexity.}
    \end{cvitems}
}
