\cventry
{Pro Hackin' Project 2023, in partnership with RIMAC Automobili}
{Concept Design of an Electric Personal Mobility Vehicle}
{Politecnico di Milano, Milan (IT) \& RIMAC Automobili, Zagreb (HR)}
{Mar 2023 - Jun 2023}
{
    Multi-phase team design competition involving students from 4 top European universities, with iterative corporate feedback sessions after each hackathon stage.
    \textbf{Selected as winning team by RIMAC Automobili engineers.}
    \newline
    \vspace{4mm}
    \begin{cvitems}
        \item {Conducted market research and user needs analysis to define target demographics and establish functional requirements for electric personal mobility concept.}
        \item {Applied systematic design methodology including functional decomposition and morphological matrix analysis to generate and evaluate multiple concept variants.}
        \item {Developed detailed \textbf{CAD models} of selected concept, defining vehicle architecture, component packaging, and manufacturing considerations.}
        \item {Performed \textbf{FEM structural analysis} to validate chassis design under operational loads and identify optimization opportunities for weight reduction.}
        \item {Conducted \textbf{FMEA (Failure Mode and Effects Analysis)} and preliminary cost estimation, evaluating design feasibility from reliability and economic perspectives.}
    \end{cvitems}
}
