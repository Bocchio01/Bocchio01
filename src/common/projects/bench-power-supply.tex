\cventry
{Personal project for hands-on electronics and power systems design}
{Design and Construction of Adjustable Bench Power Supply}
{Personal Workshop, Como (IT)}
{Feb 2023 - Feb 2023}
{
    Custom-built laboratory power supply with digital display and adjustable voltage/current regulation, integrating analog circuitry with mechanical design for educational and prototyping purposes.
    \newline
    \vspace{4mm}
    \begin{cvitems}
        \item {Designed enclosure and component mounting structure using \textbf{CAD software}, fabricating custom parts via \textbf{3D printing}.}
        \item {Assembled rectification circuit with \textbf{diode bridge} and smoothing capacitors to convert AC transformer output into stable DC supply, using salvaged transformer from recycled electronics.}
        \item {Integrated adjustable voltage and current regulation module with protection circuits to prevent overcurrent and short-circuit conditions.}
        \item {Implemented digital display functioning as built-in \textbf{multimeter} for real-time monitoring of output voltage and current during testing.}
    \end{cvitems}
}
